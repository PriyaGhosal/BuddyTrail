Himachal Pradesh Adventure Treks

Triund Trek

A beautiful 2-day trek near Dharamshala with stunning views of the Dhauladhar range.

  • Duration: 2 days
  • Difficulty: Easy to Moderate
  • Best Time: March to June, September to November
Hampta Pass Trek

A diverse 5-day trek crossing from lush Kullu Valley to the arid Spiti region.

  • Duration: 5 days
  • Difficulty: Moderate
  • Best Time: June to September
Bhrigu Lake Trek

A 4-day trek to a high-altitude glacial lake near Manali with panoramic views.

  • Duration: 4 days
  • Difficulty: Moderate to Difficult
  • Best Time: May to October
